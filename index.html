The Lounge Flys

Atlanta's Stone Temple Pilot Tribute Band

Biography

The Lounge Flys pays tribute by bringing you the the music of the Stone Temple Pilots.

Singer Matt Coleman hauntingly channels Scott Weiland as the chemistry of bassist Rob Groover, guitarist Robert Taylor, and drummer Jeff Wheeler come together to authentically reproduce the driving and melodic sounds of the one and only Stone Temple Pilots. Do not miss an opportunity to see The Lounge Flys!
