Meet the Band

Matt Coleman - Lead Vocals
Sang his first solo in church at 8 years old, and he’s never looked back. A classically trained multi-instrumentalist, he started writing and performing his own music at 15 on piano, guitar and bass. He’s gone on to play in different bands (Fabulous Nobodies, Kings on Fire, The Digits) touring venues across the country, headlining festivals, fundraisers and even the occasional house party.

Rob Groover - Bass/Vocals
Bass player, singer, song writer from 80s-2020. Played in various bands (Shadow puppets , Arm’s Akimbo.,The Saints, Loud Flower, KTO, Chris Edmond Band, Bryce Clifford Band, Fay Dafrico Band) blues projects.

Robert Taylor - Guitar/Vocals
Guitarist, singer, song writer from 80s-2020. Played in various bands (Jagg, Salem Ash, CRAIG.). Main rig is VOX AC30, '78 Les Paul Standard and 2009 TV Yellow Les Paul Jr. Special. In addition to STP, favorite bands are Soundgarden and Rival Sons.

Jeff Wheeler - Drums
Jeff Wheeler, drummer for The Lounge Flys, is active in the music scene, playing in original bands and tribute bands around the metro Atlanta area. Jeff's favorite genres are progressive rock/metal, grunge, and singer-songwriter music. In addition to STP, his favorite bands are Rush, Tool, Porcupine Tree, Ryan Adams, Opeth, Soundgarden, and Pearl Jam. Jeff gigs on a four-piece Tama Starclassic drum kit.
